Careers




Ragha Systems has openings in the following IT positions:

Job title – Software Developer

Job duties – Design and develop software and software systems to meet clients’ needs within time and cost constraints; design and execute data conversion process from multiple legacy systems to new system; research and test operating systems-level software.; implement inbound and outbound integration, custom/tailored and Cloud Connect integration formats, and Studio; generate ad-hoc reports and queries as specified and developed specifications for other reports and metrics using workday report writer including the use of calculated fields; develop date analytics and dashboards to satisfy requirements; perform system, stress/performance, parallel efforts throughout the life of the implementation; May need to work in other locations as required.

Requirement – Master’s Degree in Computer Science/Application, Electronics, Engineering, Information Systems, or related with 1 year experience.

Job title – MIS Analyst

Job duties – Analyze and evaluate clients existing software and database management systems and information technology needs to meet clients’ needs within time and cost constraints; design and develop software and computer systems to meet the project needs within time and cost constraints; develop e-Commerce, database driven web sites for clients using Windows and Java technologies; review and write software and database business systems analysis report on a framework as well as provide user training for developed systems; track system performance by performing system monitoring and analysis, and performance tuning; troubleshooting system hardware, software, networks and operating systems; secures computer system by developing system access criteria, monitoring and controlling all system access, and implementing security controls to ensure data integrity. May need to work in other locations as required.

Requirement – Bachelor’s degree in Management Information System, Information Technology, Business Administration, or related at least 5 years of experience.

Job title – IT Architect

Job duties – Design and maintain computer networks and computer systems to meet the project needs; model and test network plans prior to implementation for the portal application and work on the deployment of the solution in a client environment and code optimization; research, analyze and create specifications for computer systems and networks according to clients requirements; design and develop the systems and networks in accordance with the specifications; configure, test and debug the systems; provide ongoing maintenance and upgrade for the systems; define the development tools and environment, to provide technical support and technical quality control throughout all stages of the project; develop enterprise level software and infrastructure solutions to shape architectures consistent with platforms, roadmaps and standards for design; document the technology integration or migration strategies. May need to work in other locations as required.

Requirement – Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Electronics, Communication Engineering, Information Technology related, or its U.S. equivalent with 5 years of experience.

Job title – MIS Analyst

Job duties – Analyze and evaluate clients’ existing software and database management systems and information technology needs to meet clients’ needs within time and cost constraints; design and develop software and computer systems to meet the project needs within time and cost constraints; develop e-Commerce, database driven web sites for clients using Windows and Java technologies; review and write software and database business systems analysis report on a framework as well as provide user training for developed systems; track system performance by performing system monitoring and analysis, and performance tuning; troubleshooting system hardware, software, networks and operating systems; secures computer system by developing system access criteria, monitoring and controlling all system access, and implementing security controls to ensure data integrity. May need to work in other locations as required.

Requirement – Master’s degree in Information System, Management, Business, Communication Engineering, or related.

Job title – Business Systems Analyst

Job duties – Analyze and evaluate clients existing software and database management systems to develop various methodologies, scenarios, analysis, tracking and reporting; Collect and analyze user’s business requirements, then design, implement and test customized business system and standard operating procedures based on the analysis of the user’s requirements and business specifications; create modules as per project specifications, and provide support for system requirement deliverables, user interface specifications, application statistics reporting and business intelligence solutions; plan, analyze and estimate test resource requirements to identify risks and track project status; design and develop interactive results analysis viewers, and track quality assurance metrics to provide code coverage analysis metrics; import and export data to and from database and generate reports. May need to work in other locations as required.

Requirement – Associate’s Degree with 2 years’ experience.
